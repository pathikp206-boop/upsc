2025 Quad Leaders' Summit (India hosted) – Key Outcomes

  • Pahalgam Consensus – Strongest-ever condemnation of cross-border terrorism; first time Quad named a specific terror incident on Indian soil (Pahalgam Attack 2025)
  • MAITRI – Maritime Initiative for Training in the Indo-Pacific; India hosted inaugural workshop to train ASEAN & Pacific Island nations in EEZ monitoring via satellite
  • Quad Indo-Pacific Logistics Network – Pilot to share airlift capacity for HADR (disaster relief); precursor to military interoperability
  • Ports of the Future Partnership – Conference in Mumbai (Oct 2025); "green port" investments in Indo-Pacific to counter debt-trap diplomacy
  • Quad Critical Minerals Initiative – Diversify EV battery supply chains; reduce Chinese processing dependence
  • Cancer Moonshot – India supplied 40 million cervical cancer vaccine doses to Indo-Pacific under Quad banner

India's 'C' grade for National Accounts = "shortcomings that somewhat hamper surveillance." Grading scale: A (Adequate) → D (Serious Shortcomings).

Weaknesses Identified & Corrective Action

  • Outdated Base Year: India still uses 2011-12 (misrepresents digital economy)
  • Deflation Method: Uses WPI + Single Deflation. Should use PPI + Double Deflation
  • Data Divergence: Unexplained gaps between Production-side & Expenditure-side GDP
  • IMF Recommendation: Population Census immediately (delayed since 2021); Statistical Audit Authority; boost NSO resources
  • Corrective Action: New series Feb 2026 — GDP base year → 2022-23; CPI base year → 2024

SDR (Special Drawing Rights)

  • International reserve assets created by IMF; valued on 5 currencies
  • Currency basket: USD (42%), Euro (31%), RMB (11%), Yen (8%), Pound (8%)
  • Allocated to members in proportion to IMF share; exchangeable for hard currencies

Emergency Tools: RFI & RCF

RFI (Rapid Financing Instrument): Available to ALL IMF members. Non-concessional, short-term, low conditionality, no full program needed.
RCF (Rapid Credit Facility): For Low-Income Countries only. Concessional (zero/low interest), longer repayment, limited conditionality.
Both used for: natural disasters, conflicts, commodity price shocks, pandemics.

ParameterKey FactTrap

Agriculture Agreement — 3 Subsidy Boxes

Green Box: No distortion / no limit (e.g., R&D, food stamps) Amber Box: 5% of production (10% for developing countries) — the "De Minimis" cap Blue Box: Production-limiting subsidies — no limit

3 Pillars of Agreement on Agriculture (AoA)

1. Market Access (tariffication, reduction) 2. Domestic Support (AMS reduction, Green Box) 3. Export Subsidies (reduction/prohibition)

IMEC (India-Middle East-Europe Economic Corridor)

Launched: G20 New Delhi, September 2023 Participating: India, USA, UAE, Saudi Arabia, France, Germany, Italy, EU
Current status: Split into two realities — Eastern Leg (India-Gulf) = active; Northern Leg (Gulf-Europe via Israel) = frozen due to regional war.
  • Infrastructure: Railroads, ship-to-rail networks, road transport, electricity cable, hydrogen pipeline, high-speed data cable
  • Impact: Cuts India-Europe shipment time by 40% and costs by 30% vs Suez Canal route
  • Combined GDP of IMEC nations (incl. EU): $47 Trillion (~40% of global GDP)
  • India-UAE Virtual Trade Corridor: AI-powered customs, cuts clearance from days to hours

Jain Vows — Mahavratas vs Anuvratas

Mahavratas (Monks/Nuns)

  • Absolute ahimsa
  • Absolute truth
  • Non-stealing
  • Celibacy (Brahmacharya)
  • Non-possession

Anuvratas (Lay followers)

  • Same 5 vows but modified
  • Less strict than mahavratas
  • Adapted for householders
  • Called "small vows"
⚠ Anuvrata = Jainism (for lay followers). Never confuse with Buddhist sila or Hindu vrata.

Samkhya — Key Points (PYQ 2013)
Dualism: Purusha (consciousness/soul) + Prakriti (matter/nature) are eternally distinct.
Liberation: Achieved when Purusha recognizes itself as distinct from Prakriti (Vivekajnana).
Accepts rebirth: YES ✅ (until liberation; do NOT say Samkhya rejects rebirth).
Karma does not create new bondage after liberation, but rebirth continues until true self-knowledge.
Yuga Sequence — Standard Order
Krita (Satya) → Treta → Dvapara → Kali
Each yuga progressively shorter and more corrupt. We are currently in Kali Yuga.

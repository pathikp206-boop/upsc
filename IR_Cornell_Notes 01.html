Sevilla Forum on Debt

Forum was ANNOUNCED at FfD4 (Sevilla, June 2025) but LAUNCHED at UNCTAD 16 (Oct 2025). Don't confuse the venues.
  • Lead Country: Spain (bridge EU creditors ↔ LatAm/Africa debtors)
  • Support: UNCTAD & UN DESA
  • Mandate: Permanent dialogue space between Creditors & Global South Borrowers
  • Unlike G20 Common Framework (rigid), this focuses on "Soft Diplomacy"

Sevilla Hierarchy

LevelNameWhat it is
1Sevilla Commitment"The What" — political pledge to close $4 Trillion SDG financing gap
2Sevilla Platform for Action"The How" — 130+ initiatives list
3Sevilla Forum on Debt"The Who" — the institution (one specific initiative)

4 Key Outcomes of FfD4

  • Sevilla Forum on Debt – dialogue
  • Debt Swaps Hub – global centre for "Debt-for-Nature" swaps
  • Debt-for-Development Swap Programme – Italian-led fund (€230m)
  • Debt Pause Clause Alliance – pause repayment for 2 yrs during climate disasters (borrows from Barbados's Bridgetown Initiative)
Summary UNCTAD (1964, Geneva, 195 members). Sevilla Forum: announced FfD4 Sevilla → launched UNCTAD 16. Hierarchy: Commitment(What) → Platform(How) → Forum(Who). Debt Pause Clause = 2-yr pause on repayment during disasters.

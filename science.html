Science & Technology

01

Indian Space Programme (ISRO)

Exam Focus: ISRO missions — Chandrayaan, Mangalyaan, Aditya, GSLV/PSLV differences — Prelims favourite. Current affairs additions are crucial.
LANDMARK MISSIONS

ISRO Key Missions

  • Aryabhata (1975) — India's first satellite (Soviet launch)
  • APPLE (1981) — first geostationary communications satellite
  • IRS-1A (1988) — first Indian Remote Sensing satellite
  • Chandrayaan-1 (2008) — confirmed water molecules on Moon
  • Mangalyaan/MOM (2014) — first Asian Mars mission; cheapest Mars mission
  • Chandrayaan-3 (2023) — soft landing at Moon's south pole; first nation to do so
  • Aditya-L1 (2023) — first solar observatory; Lagrange point 1
  • Gaganyaan — India's first crewed mission (upcoming)
LAUNCH VEHICLES

Launch Vehicles

  • PSLV — Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle; workhorse; 4-stage (solid-liquid alternating)
  • GSLV Mk-II — Geosynchronous; cryogenic upper stage
  • GSLV Mk-III / LVM3 — India's heaviest; OneWeb satellite launches
  • SSLV — Small Satellite Launch Vehicle; low-cost small satellites
  • Record: PSLV-C37 — 104 satellites in single launch (2017)
NAVIGATION

Navigation & Communication

  • NavIC (IRNSS) — Indian Navigation System; 7 satellites; ±5m accuracy
  • GSAT series — communication satellites
  • RISAT — Radar Imaging Satellite (all-weather)
  • EDUSAT — education broadcast satellite
  • IN-SPACe — body regulating private space sector
02

Nuclear & Defence Technology

NUCLEAR

India's Nuclear Programme

  • DAE (1954) — Homi J. Bhabha; 3-stage programme
  • Stage 1: U-235 → Plutonium (thermal reactors)
  • Stage 2: Fast Breeder Reactors (Plutonium + Thorium)
  • Stage 3: Thorium (U-233) reactors — India's long-term goal
  • Pokhran-I (1974) — "Smiling Buddha"; Pokhran-II (1998) — "Operation Shakti"
  • Nuclear doctrine — No First Use (NFU) policy
  • BARC — Bhabha Atomic Research Centre, Trombay
DEFENCE

Defence Missiles & Systems

  • IGMDP — Integrated Guided Missile Development Programme (Dr. Kalam)
  • Agni series — ballistic missiles (I to V; Agni-V: 5000km range)
  • Prithvi — short-range surface-to-surface
  • BrahMos — supersonic cruise missile (India-Russia joint); Mach 2.8
  • Akash — surface-to-air; indigenous
  • Tejas — Light Combat Aircraft; HAL made; IAF inducted
BIOTECHNOLOGY

Biotechnology & Health

  • Covaxin — India's indigenous COVID vaccine by Bharat Biotech + ICMR
  • Covishield — AstraZeneca/Oxford vaccine; manufactured by SII
  • Bt Cotton — only GM crop approved in India (2002)
  • GM Mustard (DMH-11) — approved but controversial
  • CRISPR-Cas9 — gene editing; potential in treating genetic diseases
  • DBT — Department of Biotechnology (under Ministry of S&T)
03

Emerging Technologies

AI & COMPUTING

Artificial Intelligence

  • National AI Strategy — NITI Aayog (#AIForAll)
  • IndiaAI Mission — 2024; GPU compute infrastructure
  • AI applications: healthcare (imaging), agriculture (crop prediction), governance
  • Concerns: bias, deepfakes, job displacement, data privacy
  • PDPB / DPDP Act (2023) — India's data protection law
ENERGY

Renewable Energy

  • India — 3rd largest solar capacity globally
  • National Solar Mission — 100 GW target by 2022 (part of NAPCC)
  • ISA — International Solar Alliance (India-France joint initiative, HQ Gurugram)
  • PM-Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana — rooftop solar
  • National Hydrogen Mission — green hydrogen economy
  • Green Hydrogen — produced by electrolysis using renewable energy
DIGITAL INDIA

Digital Technology

  • UPI — Unified Payment Interface; NPCI-governed; largest globally
  • Aadhaar — 12-digit biometric ID; 1.4 billion+ enrolled
  • ONDC — Open Network for Digital Commerce
  • Account Aggregator framework — financial data sharing
  • 5G rollout in India — 2022; 6G roadmap by DoT
  • Semiconductor Mission — India's chip fabrication push

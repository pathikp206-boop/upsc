Summary — golden threads for UPSC
Timeline spine: Stockholm 1972 (UNEP) → Rio 1992 (UNFCCC + CBD) → Kyoto 1997 (binding Annex-I only) → Bali 2007 (road map) → Copenhagen 2009 ($100B pledge) → Paris 2015 (universal NDCs) → Glasgow 2021 (Paris rulebook + net-zero pledges) → Dubai 2023 (1st GST + L&D fund) → Baku 2024 ($300B NCQG) → Belém 2025 (Article 6 operationalized + 4×fuel pledge).
Critical traps: Montreal = only universal treaty · Kigali under Montreal not UNFCCC · USA not in CBD · CITES ≠ habitat protection · Ramsar covers all wetland types incl rice fields · None of 20 Aichi Targets met · CDM hosted most projects in India · 2% CERs→Adaptation Fund (Kyoto); 5% A6.4ERs→Adaptation Fund (Paris Art.6.4) · COP-26 coal "phase-down" pushed by India · India HFC freeze 2028 (latest group).
India numbers: Net-zero 2070 · 96 Ramsar sites · 45% GDP emission intensity cut · 50% non-fossil electricity (both by 2030) · ₹81,664 Cr NBSAP · ₹20,000 Cr CCUS · 26 Mha land restoration target · HFC 80%+ cut by 2047.

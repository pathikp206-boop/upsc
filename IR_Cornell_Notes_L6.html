Russian System Quick Ladder

SummaryS-550 = ASAT/space (counters X-37B, 2025). S-500 = ICBMs+LEO satellites, first regiment Moscow 2025. S-550 ≠ S-500 ≠ Su-57. Su-57 Felon = 5th Gen jet; India withdrew from FGFA 2018. Algeria first export customer for Su-57.

Israel's Layered Air Defense Architecture

SystemRange / LayerFunction2025-26 Note / Trap
Iron BeamShort-range (DEW)Laser Defense. 100kW lasers destroy rockets/drones for ~$2 per shot.Game Changer: Officially deployed Dec 2025 alongside Iron Dome. Neutralizes cheap drone swarms without wasting $50k missiles. DEW = Directed Energy Weapon.
Iron DomeShort-range (Kinetic, 0-70km)Intercepts rockets, mortars, dronesUsed in Op True Promise 3 (June 2025) — overwhelmed by ~500 Iranian missiles. Complemented by Iron Beam now.
David's SlingMedium-range (40-300km)Fills gap between Iron Dome (short) and Arrow (long). "Magic Wand."Successfully intercepted "Khaibar-Shekan" (Iran's advanced ballistic missile).
Arrow-3Long-range / Exo-atmosphericDestroys ballistic missiles in space (hypersonic speeds). 1600km range.Combat Proven: First ever combat use of space-warfare interceptors — shot down Houthi missiles from Yemen. Iran's Fattah-2 was designed specifically to counter Arrow-3.
Barak MagenNaval (Sea-based)Naval Iron Dome. Ship-borne interceptor on Sa'ar 6 corvettes.Protected Israel's offshore gas rigs (Karish + Leviathan) from Hezbollah drone attacks. Energy security application.
Iron StingPrecision MortarLaser-guided 120mm mortar. Hits specific rooms/windows without collapsing buildings.Urban Warfare tool. Used in Gaza. "Surgical precision" = minimizes civilian collateral.
Order from ground-up: Iron Beam (DEW, close-in) → Iron Dome (0-70km) → David's Sling (40-300km) → Arrow-2 (Endo-atmospheric) → Arrow-3 (Exo-atmospheric, space).
SummaryIsrael's layered shield: Iron Beam (laser, $2/shot, Dec 2025) + Iron Dome (kinetic, short) + David's Sling (medium, "Magic Wand") + Arrow-3 (exo-atmospheric, space, combat proven vs Houthis). Barak Magen = naval version on Sa'ar 6 corvettes. Iron Sting = precision mortar for urban warfare.

Drishti-10 = first private sector (Adani) MALE UAV inducted by Navy. C-295 = first Made-in-India military aircraft (Vadodara, Sept 2026). AH-64E Apache = Indian Army 451 Sqn Jodhpur.

Typhon SystemUSAMid-Range Ground Launcher. Fires Tomahawk (Land Attack) and SM-6 (Anti-Ship) missiles."Missile Crisis of the East": Permanently deployed in Philippines (Luzon Island) in 2025 — provoked China, as it can strike the Chinese mainland. Analogous to NATO missiles in Europe.

Fattah-2IranHypersonic Missile. Features a maneuverable re-entry vehicle (MaRV).Escalation Tool: Unveiled as direct response to Israel's Arrow-3 shield. MaRV = can change direction during re-entry, making interception harder.
SummaryHQ-19 (China) = exo-atmospheric ABM (like THAAD); Pakistan inducting to counter Agni missiles. Typhon (USA) = Tomahawk + SM-6 launcher; in Philippines Luzon, can hit Chinese mainland. FrankenSAM = Ukraine hybrid (Western missile + Soviet launcher). VAMPIRE = anti-drone kit vs Shahed-136. Fattah-2 (Iran) = hypersonic + MaRV, counters Arrow-3.

Op Swords of Iron = overall Gaza war name. Op Epic Fury/Lion's Roar (Feb 2026) = most intense US-Israel vs Iran escalation; regime change objective.

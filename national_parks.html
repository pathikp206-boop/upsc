💧 Special Ramsar Comparisons — UPSC Tested

Lake Records

  • Largest freshwater lake → Wular Lake, J&K
  • Largest lake NE India → Loktak, Manipur
  • Longest lake → Vembanad, Kerala
  • Largest coastal lagoon → Chilika, Odisha
  • Largest inland saltwater → Sambhar, Rajasthan
  • Smallest Ramsar site → Renuka, HP (20 ha)

Montreux Record Status

  • Currently ON record: Loktak Lake (Manipur)
  • Currently ON record: Keoladeo NP (Rajasthan)
  • REMOVED (restored): Chilika Lake (Odisha)
  • Meteorite crater lake: Lonar Lake, Maharashtra
  • High-altitude Black-necked Crane: Tso Moriri, Ladakh
